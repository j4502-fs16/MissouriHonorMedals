Banquet

The Missouri Honor Medal Banquet climactially wraps up a day of master classes with a celebratory dinner. The banquet honors the nominees with a formal three-course dinner. Each medalist is then introduced by the dean or provost, presented with a medal and invited to give a speech.

Before and after the banquet, students and faculty can interact with the recipients in a more conversational atmosphere.

To RSVP for the banquet, print and fill out the invitation. Then submit it with a cash, check or credit payment of $40 (students) or $50 to Suzette Heiman at 120 Neff Hall.
