Missouri Honor Medal

Events

Each year as part of Missouri Honor Medal festivities, individual recipients and representatives from organizations hold master classes to share their expertise and offer insights into advancing inclusion and democracy in the field of journalism. Sessions begin at the Missouri School of Journalism at 9 a.m. Tuesday, Oct. 17 and are free and open to the public. Master classes are held all day preceding the Honor Medal Banquet that evening.

The full day's schedule of events:

“The Power of Saying No: How to Gain Influence as a Journalist”

9-9:50 a.m. in Fred W. Smith Forum, 200 Reynolds Journalism Institute

Medalist: Janice Min, Co-President and Chief Creative Officer of The Hollywood Reporter-Billboard Media Group

“From Anger to Empathy: Channeling Emotion in Opinion Writing and News Reporting”

9:30-10:45 a.m.
